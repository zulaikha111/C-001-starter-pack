1) CITY OVERVIEW
====================================================================

City Name: Rivergate City
Region: South Marlin Coast, Province of Alderwick
Country: The Commonwealth of Lydiana 

Short description
Rivergate City is a mid-sized river-and-bay city built where the Ember River meets Pelican Bay.
It is known for its salt-marsh boardwalks, light industry, and an unusually dense network of commuter rail.
Local news often describes Rivergate as “a working port with a university spine.”

Key figures (estimated)
Population (city proper): 412,800
Population (greater metro): 598,200
Median age: 36.9
Households: 166,500
Area: 146 km²
Population density: 2,828 people/km²
Languages: Lydian (primary), Coast Pidgin, Aramite
Primary industries: logistics, ship repair, green manufacturing, higher education, tourism, and digital services
Emergency number: 999 (Lydiana standard)
City information line: 311
Visitor hotline: +99 20 555 0110

3) GEOGRAPHY, CLIMATE, WEATHER, AND SEASONS
====================================================================

Geography
Rivergate sits on a low-lying delta with a barrier peninsula to the south.
The Ember River splits into three channels near downtown.
The city’s built-up areas rise to the north onto the Juniper Ridge.

Notable natural features
- Pelican Bay Estuary
- Ember River Marshes
- Juniper Ridge (highest point: 212 m above sea level)
- Stonebridge Gorge (urban creek canyon)
- Saltgrass Flats (a protected wetland on the Seabird Peninsula)

Seasons (southern hemisphere)
Summer: Dec–Feb
Autumn: Mar–May
Winter: Jun–Aug
Spring: Sep–Nov

Typical climate profile
Summer highs: 26–32°C
Summer lows: 16–20°C
Winter highs: 12–17°C
Winter lows: 5–9°C
Annual rainfall: 760 mm
Windiest months: Aug–Oct
UV index: Very high in summer (often 8–11)

Current week weather snapshot ( example)
- Fri 16 Jan: 30°C max, 18°C min, coastal breeze 22 km/h, chance of showers 20%
- Sat 17 Jan: 28°C max, 17°C min, humid morning, chance of showers 35%
- Sun 18 Jan: 31°C max, 19°C min, afternoon thunder risk 25%
- Mon 19 Jan: 27°C max, 16°C min, cooler change, chance of showers 40%
- Tue 20 Jan: 25°C max, 15°C min, clear evening, chance of showers 10%

Weather advisories used by the city
Heat Alert (Amber): forecast above 33°C or overnight above 22°C
Wind Alert: gusts above 45 km/h near the bay
Flood Watch: tide + rain + river surge conditions

Common microclimates
- Seabird Peninsula is 2–4°C cooler than Juniper Heights on most summer afternoons.
- Northbank is often foggy at dawn due to river evaporation.
- East Marrow Industrial gets hotter because of wide asphalt yards and lower tree cover.

Seasonal travel note used by the tourism board
“Pack layers in spring. Coastal winds arrive quickly. The ridge stays cooler at night.”

9) PUBLIC TRANSPORT: TRAINS, LINES, STATIONS, AND TIMETABLES
====================================================================

Transit operator
Rivergate Transit Authority (RTA)
Contact: 311 then “Transit”
Lost property: +99 20 555 0199 
Smartcard: “GateCard”
Standard fare (adult, peak): 3.60 credits per trip (zones apply)

Rail network overview
Rivergate has 4 urban rail lines and 1 regional connector.
Trains are electric multiple units (EMU) in the urban network.

Major stations
- Harbor Central (CBD, Old Wharf)
- Northbank Junction (Northbank)
- Juniper Ridge Station (Juniper Heights)
- Seabird Terminal (Seabird Peninsula)
- Mapleworks Yard (Mapleworks)
- East Marrow Freight & Platform (limited passenger)
- Westfield Commons Station (Westfield Commons)
- Stonebridge Gorge Station (Stonebridge)

Urban rail lines
A) Harbor Line (HL) – Westfield ↔ Harbor Central ↔ Seabird Terminal
Key stops: Westfield Commons, Northbank Junction, Harbor Central, Tideway Park, Seabird Terminal

B) Ridge Line (RL) – Northbank Junction ↔ Juniper Ridge ↔ Stonebridge Gorge
Key stops: Northbank Junction, Cedarline, Juniper Ridge, Quarry Bend, Stonebridge Gorge

C) Foundry Line (FL) – Harbor Central ↔ Mapleworks Yard ↔ East Marrow Platform
Key stops: Harbor Central, Copper Mill, Mapleworks Yard, Foundry Gate, East Marrow Platform

D) Bay Loop (BL) – Harbor Central ↔ Old Wharf (short loop) ↔ Harbor Central
Key stops: Harbor Central, Lantern Street, Dockmaster Quay, Harbor Central

Regional connector
E) Alderlink (ALX) – Harbor Central ↔ Alderwick City (regional capital)
Travel time: ~58 minutes end-to-end
